Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Nuvara collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use nuvara.co.uk.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Email address — when you join our waitlist or newsletter
  • Stock search queries — tickers or company names you search for (not linked to your identity)

We also collect certain information automatically: browser type, pages visited, referring website, and general location (country level only).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Send you our newsletter and product updates
  • Improve and develop the Service
  • Understand how users interact with the scanner
  • Respond to your enquiries

3. Cookie Policy

We use cookies to improve your experience. Essential cookies are required for the site to function. Analytics cookies help us understand usage patterns using anonymised data only. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with: Beehiiv (email provider), Netlify (hosting), Financial Modelling Prep (financial data — search queries only, no personal data), and when required by law.

5. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@nuvara.co.uk.

6. Contact Us

Questions? Contact us at hello@nuvara.co.uk.
