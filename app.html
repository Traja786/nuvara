☪️ Zakat on Investments
How zakat on investments works: Zakat is 2.5% of the total value of your halal investments, payable once per lunar year (hawl) if your total wealth exceeds the nisab threshold. The nisab is based on the value of 85g of gold or 595g of silver — whichever is lower.
📊
